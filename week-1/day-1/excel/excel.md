# Excel Learning Notes

## Total Sales Column
Created a new column `total_sales` to calculate daily totals.

Formula:
=SUM(B2:I2)

Copied formula using fill handle.

---

## Date Column Fix
Problem: Dates showed #####
Fix: Expanded column width (double-click column edge).

## Summary Calculations

Total Sales:
=SUM(O2:O2107)

Average:
=AVERAGE(O2:O2107)

Maximum:
=MAX(O2:O2107)

Minimum:
=MIN(O2:O2107)

## Formula Corrections

Wrong:
=SUM(2:2107)

=SUM(O2:O2107)

## Sorting

Sorted `total_sales`:
Largest to Smallest

Steps:
1. Click filter arrow on `total_sales`
2. Choose `Sort Largest to Smallest`
