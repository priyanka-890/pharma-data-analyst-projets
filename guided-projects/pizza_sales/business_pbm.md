# üçï Pizza Sales Analysis ‚Äì Business Problem

## üìå Business Context

The management of a pizza restaurant chain wants to better understand its sales performance and customer ordering behavior.  
They are looking for insights that can help improve revenue, identify high- and low-performing products, and optimize operations.

The data team has been asked to analyze historical pizza sales data and present findings in a clear and actionable format.

---

## üéØ Business Objectives

The objectives of this analysis are to:

- Evaluate overall sales performance
- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Identify best-selling and underperforming pizzas
- Analyze ordering trends by date and time
- Support data-driven business decisions using insights and visuals

---

## ‚ùì Key Business Questions

The analysis aims to answer the following business questions:

- How much revenue is the business generating?
- How much does a customer spend per order on average?
- How many pizzas and orders are being sold?
- Which pizzas and categories perform best and worst?
- When do customers place the most orders (daily and hourly)?
- What pizza sizes and categories are most popular?

---

## üìê Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Based on the business questions, the following KPIs were identified:

1. **Total Revenue**  
   Measures overall business income from pizza sales.

2. **Average Order Value**  
   Indicates how much customers spend per order on average.

3. **Total Pizzas Sold**  
   Represents total demand for pizzas.

4. **Total Orders**  
   Shows the volume of customer transactions.

5. **Average Pizzas per Order**  
   Helps understand customer buying behavior per order.

---

To effectively answer the business questions, the following visualizations were created:

- **Purpose:** Identify daily patterns and fluctuations in order volume

- **Purpose:** Identify peak ordering hours

### 3. Sales Distribution by Pizza Category
- **Purpose:** Understand which categories contribute most to revenue

### 4. Sales Distribution by Pizza Size

- **Chart Type:** Pie chart

### 5. Total Pizzas Sold by Category
- **Purpose:** Compare performance across pizza categories  
- **Chart Type:** Funnel chart

### 6. Top 5 Best-Selling Pizzas
- **Purpose:** Identify high-performing products  
- **Chart Type:** Bar chart

### 7. Bottom 5 Worst-Selling Pizzas
- **Purpose:** Identify underperforming products  
- **Chart Type:** Bar chart
